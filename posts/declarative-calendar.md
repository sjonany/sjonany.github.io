# Declarative Calendar
I‘m a big believer in habits, and am uh … pretty [neurotic](https://medium.com/@sjonany/how-i-spend-my-time-aba6bdc3388) about organizing my time. I want my habits to be easy to manage, version-controlled so I can see how my time-spending changes, and easy to reflect on with an LLM.

So! With the power vested in me through vibes, I finally got around to creating a tool that does exactly this — You have a [yaml file](https://github.com/sjonany/Declarative-Calendar-demo-/blob/main/habits.yaml) as the source of truth, and a UI for viewing it, and syncing it to a dedicated Google Calendar.
